Work in Progress

Endangered is a free-to-play Post-Apocalyptic MMORPG created by BeccyTurner in 2019. It was created on the Roblox engine, scripted in Lua programming Language.

Currently, Endangered is on it's early stage of development. Endangered will features many things that are typically available on MMORPG such as leveling, crafting, combat, npc, trading, events, and many more. However, what made Endangered different from other MMORPG is that players are able to govern one of 6 factions that they can play on.

Endangered factions consist of Assassin, Bandit, Civilian, Cyborg, Knight and Pirate. Each faction will have their own version of government for example: Civilian would have democracy, Knight will have monarchy, Pirate would be anarchy, etc. Player may choose to join the politics and rise to the top or stay away from politics and play casually.

Game Description

Endangered is an open world game. Being introduced at a time after war, and creation, several districts are going head to head over territory and strength. Given the abilities to join six different districts and hunt, lead and build your way to the top, this game will keep you entertained. There are limitless ways to play this game, and will be constantly experiencing updates. With many challenges ahead such as bandits, cyborgs, pirates, and creatures, will you be able to rise above the rest?
