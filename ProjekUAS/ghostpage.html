Ghost is a free-to-play Survival Game created by BeccyTurner. It was created on the Roblox engine, scripted in Lua programming Language.

Currently, the status of Ghost development is unknown due to the developer themselves hasn't publicly announce the game or open the development for testing.

Game Description

Someone amongst the crowd is not like the others. The ghost is given the ability to possess players, making them look like someone who they are not. A flash of lightning signifies when the ghost has possessed someone, use this as an advantage to know when someone can be trusted. Zappers are given to survivors to use to defeat off the ghost and protect themselves, but be careful, every possession results in the ghosts health being restored to max. Unlock perks by leveling up and purchasing them, increasing the abilities in game to help with ghost, or survivor gameplay. Deception is key, remember to trust not even yours friends.
